Pro.Yuanlongping

The father of the hybridrice

Here is a time line of Pro.Yuan's life:

  • 1930-Born in Beijing,China.
  • 1949-Graduated from juior high,Yuan determined to be an agricultural scientist.
  • 1953-After college,Yuan devoted himself to solving the problem of lacking food.
  • 1960-Yuan discoverd a natural rice with a special appearance.He then claimed that it could be the natural hybrid rice.
  • 1968-After thousands of tests, he finally found the right one.
  • 1973-YUAN made a great progress in the hybrid rice area.
  • 1987-Awarded by the The United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization.
  • 2000His latest research rice's production capacity has reached 700kg per mu.
  • 2004-Receives the world food prize.
  • 2019-Receives the National Medal and national honorary title of the people's Republic of China.
  • 2021-dies at the age of 91.
